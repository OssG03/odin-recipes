Homestyle Smash Burger

Ingredients

  • 80/20 Lean/Fat Ground Beef (1 lb)
  • Kosher Salt
  • American Cheese (4 Slices)
  • Burger Buns (4)

Steps

  1. Divide meat into four loosely formed balls
  2. Heat griddle to 375F, or until water droplets immediately boil upon contact
  3. Place ball of meat on griddle
  4. Cover with parchment paper and press down with spatula or bacon press
  5. Get patty as thin as possible
  6. Sprinkle with salt to taste
  7. Flip once crust has formed, cover with cheese
  8. Cook for 1-2 more minutes
  9. Place on toasted bun
  10. Enjoy
