Country Style Ribs

Ingredients

  • Pork Ribs (Full Rack)
  • Dry Rub
  • Preferred BBQ Sauce

Steps

  1. Pull silverskin off of back end of ribs, trim excess fat
  2. Put an even coating of dry rub on entirety of rack
  3. Put in fridge for at least 1 hour
  4. If using gas grill, turn on to lowest setting, for charcoal or pellet, start small fire opposite of where ribs will sit
  5. Place ribs on grill and let cook for 3-3 1/2 hours covered
  6. Baste rack with sauce every half hour
  7. Once finished, let ribs rest for 15 minutes
  8. Enjoy
