Restaurant Quality Steak

Ingredients

  • Butter (unsalted)
  • Kosher Salt
  • Pepper (Ground)
  • Cut of Steak (Preferred Cut)
  • Thyme (Four Fresh Sprigs)
  • Minced Garlic (1 Tablespoon)

Steps

  1. Pat dry steak, season evenly with kosher salt and pepper
  2. In preheated cast iron pan, put tablespoon of butter
  3. Wait until butter begins to bubble, then place steak in pan
  4. Cook steak, ensuring crust forms
  5. Once crust has formed, flip steak and sear on opposite side until desired doneness is reached
  6. Once cooked, turn off heat and put in another tablespoon of butter, garlic, and thyme
  7. Let butter melt, then spoon on steak repeatedly for 1 minute
  8. Remove steak from pan, plate, and let rest for 5 minutes
  9. Enjoy
